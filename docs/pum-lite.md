# PUM Lite

**PUM Lite** is the free, streamlined edition of the PUM app family. It delivers the core solo RPG experience for the Plot Unfolding Machine—storytelling, journaling, random tables, and plot tracks—without the advanced world-building and power-user tools found in [PUM Companion](pum-companion.md).

PUM Lite is ideal if you want to start playing quickly, keep things simple, and focus on your story rather than managing a large compendium of entities and files.

## Key Features

- **Unlimited Stories:** Play without story or character limits
- **Random Tables:** Create simple and weighted random tables
- **PUM Plot Tracks:** Control the pace and structure of your games
- **Game Creation Wizard:** Set up new games and brainstorm from scratch
- **Analog Play:** Use your own physical dice and handwriting alongside the app
- **Episode Continuation:** Carry characters and plot tracks into new games
- **PDF Story Export:** Export your journal as a PDF (with a subtle watermark)
- **Save Game Export:** Save, share, and back up games in `.pumc` format
- **Global Search:** Find entities and journal text quickly (Ctrl+F / Ctrl+Space)
- **Image Viewer:** View images attached to entities
- **Offline Friendly:** Core features work without an internet connection

---

## PUM Lite vs PUM Companion

Both apps share the same solo RPG foundation, but they target different levels of depth. PUM Lite keeps the essentials; PUM Companion adds advanced tables, multi-vault organization, PDF tools, cloud sync, and optional DLC expansions.

For a full side-by-side breakdown of every feature, see **[PUM Lite vs PUM Companion — Feature Parity](pum-lite-vs-companion.md)**.
